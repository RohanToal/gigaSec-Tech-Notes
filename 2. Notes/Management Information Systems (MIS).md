# management information systems (MIS)
Created:  [[2022-10-01]]

---
Helps managers/supervisor 
    ensures all the work is aligned with the organization's needs. 


The MIS pulls 
1. Transactional data from various Transactional Processing Systems 
2. Compiles the information
3. Presents it in reports and displays.
The resulting reports can be produced hourly, monthly, annually.
