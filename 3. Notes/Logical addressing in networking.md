# Logical addressing in networking
Created:  [[2022-07-05]]
Tags: #fleeting 

---
Provides 
- order to networks
- categorising them 
- and allowing us to properly sort them. 


Most common form of logical addressing is the 
- IPV4 format, (i.e 192.168.1.1 common address for home router).
